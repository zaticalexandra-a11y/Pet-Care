Frequently Asked Questions ❓

Here are some common questions about pet care, along with simple and practical answers.

How often should I feed my pet?

Most adult dogs and cats should be fed 1–2 times per day. Puppies, kittens, and small pets may need more frequent meals depending on their age and dietary needs.

How often should I take my pet to the vet?

Pets should visit the vet at least once a year for a general check-up. Older pets or those with health issues may need more frequent visits.

Do all pets need vaccinations?

Yes, most pets require vaccinations to protect against serious diseases. The exact schedule depends on the type of animal, age and local veterinary guidelines.

How much exercise does my pet need?

Dogs usually need daily walks and playtime, cats need interactive play sessions and small pets need safe space to move and explore regularly.

How do I know if my pet is sick?

Common signs include loss of appetite, vomiting, diarrhea, tiredness, sudden behavior changes or unusual weight loss. If you notice these signs, consult a veterinarian.

Can I feed my pet human food?

Some human foods are safe in small amounts, but many are harmful or toxic to pets. Always check with a vet before sharing human food with your pet.
