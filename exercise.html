Pet Exercise 🎾

Exercise keeps pets physically fit and mentally active. Activities such as walks, games and climbing help reduce stress and improve overall wellbeing.

Dogs

Dogs need daily exercise such as walks, running, fetch or agility games. The amount of exercise depends on breed, age and energy level. Active breeds may need 1–2 hours daily, while smaller or older dogs may need shorter sessions.

Cats

Cats benefit from short but frequent play sessions using toys, climbing structures, and interactive games. Exercise helps indoor cats prevent obesity and reduces boredom-related behavior problems.

Small Pets

Small pets such as rabbits, guinea pigs, and hamsters need safe space to move and explore. Exercise wheels, tunnels, and supervised floor time help keep them active and healthy.

Benefits of Regular Exercise

  • Maintains a healthy weight
  • Improves heart and muscle strength
  • Reduces anxiety and stress
  • Prevents destructive behavior
  • Supports mental stimulation and happiness
