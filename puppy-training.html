Puppy Training 🐶

Puppy training should start early using patience and positive reinforcement. Consistency, rewards and socialization help puppies develop good behavior and confidence.

Basic Commands

Start with simple commands such as sit, stay, come, and leave it. Use short training sessions (5–10 minutes) to keep the puppy focused and avoid frustration.

Positive Reinforcement

Reward good behavior with treats, praise, or toys. Avoid punishment, as it can lead to fear and confusion. Consistent rewards help puppies understand what is expected of them.

House Training

Establish a regular schedule for feeding and bathroom breaks. Take your puppy outside frequently, especially after eating, drinking, or sleeping, to reinforce good habits.

Socialization

Introduce your puppy to different people, environments, sounds, and other animals at an early age. Proper socialization helps prevent fear and aggression later in life.

Consistency and Patience

Training takes time, and puppies learn best through repetition and routine. Stay consistent with rules and commands to avoid confusion.
